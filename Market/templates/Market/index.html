Purpose
This is Hermes Exchange. I created Hermes Exchange as a fantasy stock market I wanted when I was learning about the stock market. I hope that this product is easy for you to use and you can have fun learning about how the most complex system humans have ever created works.
Firms
Firms are lobbies that users can use to see how they are compared to their friends. Firms maybe for learning groups, or may include the most talented traders already in the official market.
